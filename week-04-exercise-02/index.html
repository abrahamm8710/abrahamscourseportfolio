My Favorite Video Games

This is my collection of my favorite video games. My collection includes many games from across popular franchises, it includes video games that were released lately or were released a while back in the 2000s. My favorite video games mean a lot to me. My collection will include 6 different video games that are considered my favorites.

Shadow The Hedgehog 2005

Game | 2005 | Shadow The Hedgehog 2005

Shadow The Hedgehog is one of my favorite games that I have played. This game was released in 2005 for the PlayStation 2, GameCube, and Xbox. It is a spinoff of the Sonic The Hedgehog series, where Shadow is the main character in this game. I like this game a lot because this game allowed Shadow to use a gun and drive his iconic motorcycle. This game combined 3D fast-paced platforming while using third person shooter elements. I found it very cool as a kid to play as Shadow while holding a gun going through obstacles, I also liked how this game had a more serious and darker tone, with mild language, and slightly more violence than previous Sonic games. It was also the first Sonic Game to ever allow a character to use guns in a game, which I found pretty cool. I own this game on the Playstation, and thats why it is one of my favorite video games.

Sonic Racing Crossworlds

Game | 2025 | Kart Racing | Sonic Team

I like this game because it is also a racing game and it involves Sonic characters such as Sonic, Shadow, and Silver the Hedgehog. I like the racing mechanics in this game, I like how on the first lap, you race in one world, and then in the second lap, you are racing on a completely different world by going through a ring portal, then on the final lap you are back to the first world on lap 1. I like this feature for Sonic Racing Crossworlds because this changes your strategies and predictability for races, because it makes you prepare for any possible outcome especially in online matches where it can get so chaotic, this aspect makes the game very fun for me to enjoy because it adds challenge and tough competition. This game is part of my collection because of the insane amount of vehicle cusotmization it contains and the huge roster of playable characters it provides.
